Dahlonega Hillside Cottages

Private cottages with mountain-facing porches designed for slower, scenic date weekends.

Dahlonega Hillside Cottages is a strong option for couples who want more space than a standard hotel room. Each cottage has a porch setup for morning coffee and sunset downtime between winery outings.

The property sits far enough from traffic to feel secluded while still keeping vineyard routes practical. It works especially well for two-night trips with one day dedicated to tastings and another for slow local exploring.
